Who We Are

Founded in 1995, our zoo is dedicated to conservation, education, and unforgettable wildlife experiences. We strive to inspire love and respect for animals through meaningful encounters and learning opportunities.

With over 80 species from around the world, we are committed to providing excellent care and creating a safe, enriching environment for both animals and visitors.

Conservation Efforts

We actively support global conservation programs and partner with wildlife organizations to protect endangered species. Our breeding initiatives help restore threatened populations while our habitats are designed to mirror natural environments.
